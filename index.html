About Me

I was born during one rainy midnight, December 1st, 1996. For years we moved around the country and all that had remained with me during that time was my family and a laptop. After spending an unreasonable amount of time playing video games during childhood, I noticed that I wanted to create the games that I loved so much to play. I love creating and producing things I conjure up in my own imagination; by combining my love for video games and my passion for programming, I set myself on a course to become a software engineer and game designer on the side.
As a side effect of the life I chose, from time to time I do awesome illustrations and drawings too!
